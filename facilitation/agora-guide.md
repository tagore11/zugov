# Daily Agora — Facilitation Guide

## What This Is

The Daily Agora is the human deliberation layer of ZuGov. It runs after the Grounding Engine report is published and before the MACI vote opens.

Duration: **45 minutes. Non-negotiable.**

A session that runs long signals a poorly structured discussion, not a rich one.

---

## Format

### 00:00 – 10:00 | Report Reading
- Facilitator reads the Grounding Engine report aloud (or asks a member to read)
- Highlights 3 most critical findings
- No discussion yet — this is reading time

### 10:00 – 35:00 | Open Deliberation

**Rules:**
- No person speaks twice in a row
- Every claim should reference a Pol.is statement or Grounding Engine finding
- Facilitator may time-box speakers (3 minutes max per turn)
- Facilitator does not advocate for any position

**Facilitator prompts (when discussion stalls):**
- "What does the report say about this assumption?"
- "Has anyone seen this resolved differently in another community?"
- "Who hasn't spoken yet and might see this differently?"

### 35:00 – 45:00 | Consensus Check

Facilitator asks: **"Does anyone have a blocking objection to moving to a vote?"**

- **No blocking objection** → MACI vote opens
- **Blocking objection exists** → Document the objection. Add Day 4 (second Agora). Objection must be specific and addressable, not a general disagreement.

---

## Facilitator Selection

- Selected by rotation for each decision cycle
- Any member with a declared interest in the proposal **recuses themselves**
- A backup facilitator is designated before each cycle
- Facilitation does not require technical knowledge — it requires neutrality

---

## What the Facilitator Does NOT Do

- Advocate for any outcome
- Summarize the "correct" interpretation of the report
- Rush the consensus check
- Allow the same person to dominate

---

## Recording

All Agora sessions are recorded with community consent. Recordings are available to all community members. Recordings are NOT published externally without explicit community vote.

---

## Warning Signs

| Sign | What It Means |
|------|--------------|
| Session runs over 45 min | Proposal is not ready for vote — send back for revision |
| Same 2-3 people speaking only | Facilitator should explicitly invite others |
| "AI said so" as an argument | Remind community: report is epistemic input, not authority |
| Blocking objection used as delay tactic | Third Agora is never added — blocking must be substantive |
